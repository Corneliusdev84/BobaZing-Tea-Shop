What is BOBAZING?

We're glad you asked! BOBAZING started out as a small little Boba shop here in Gilbert and soon grew to be a place where anyone can stop by for delicious cold drinks and milk teas. You can even customize your own drink! Just ask us how! Leave us a 5 star review on GOOGLE and earn a free boba drink! We have an exclusive Cold-Brewing tea formula that gives our drinks the best taste; cold, smooth, and refreshing in each and every cup. We stand for the highest quality in our teas and our cold brew formulas which we stand by and we display the teas before our guests with confidence like no other tea houses.
